Last updated: January 2026

We collect the details you give us directly — your name, company name, phone number, email, and mailing address — when you fill out a form, request a quote or setup, apply for a position, or otherwise contact us.

We use your information to respond to inquiries, provide our transportation and logistics services, process applications, communicate with you (by phone, email, or text where you've consented), and improve your experience.

We never sell your personal information. We may share it with trusted vendors who help us run our business, or disclose it when required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards — including encryption and access controls — to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

You can request to access, correct, or delete your information at any time, and unsubscribe from promotional messages whenever you like. To stop text messages, reply STOP to any message. To exercise these rights, contact us below.
